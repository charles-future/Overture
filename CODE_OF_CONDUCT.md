# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a positive experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviors include:**
- Being respectful and inclusive
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards others

**Unacceptable behaviors include:**
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to [support@trysixth.com](mailto:support@trysixth.com). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
